About Us

Wachsen Gedeihen was founded on May 5 2020 by 3 friends. Departing from real life, starting with a hobby of drawing and design, looking at social life, social issues, and Indonesian culture. In the end, the three of us applied it through a clothing brand with the aim that Indonesian people, especially young Indonesians, could be more proud of wearing products made in Indonesia that were not inferior to foreign products so that they could be recognized both at home and abroad.

Wachsen Gedeihen is taken from German which means "Growing and Developing". Many ask "why use German, even though you guys are local products?". We also answer that this is just a name, because research at that time, many growing sentences were already widely used. The meaning is that we hope that this wachsen can continue to grow and develop as a clothing brand made by the nation's children that can be recognized and continues to work to produce quality local products. Wachsen Gedeihen is not just a mere brand name, but also our identity as a brand based in Bogor - West Java, Indonesia.
